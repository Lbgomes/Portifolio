My Role

I was brought up to this project later on its development; another team was working on it, while the client was not satisfied with the lack of details in reproducing the design into UI. Management then asked me to step up as they needed someone that is more comfortable in reproducing design into pixel perfect UI in a time sensitive context. In summary, my role in this project was to make fine adjustments to the responsivenes and refine components to fit an identical representation of the desired UI. It was very rewarding to step up in that project as a reference of frontend best practices, in which I had very little context of the project or its codebase, and still assisted them in making the client satisfied.
