The project

Lounge is a platform for all the latest and greatest about partying and drinking

- You interact live with all kinds of contentt like polls and surveys
- Workshops about gastronomy, beer, career, business and behavior to awake your creativity
- Exclusive shows and lives of your favorite artists

My Role

I was allocated in this project as a solo dev, because management needed a developer with good knowledge around layout development - and it needed to be someone that they'd have enough confidence to lead the project alone for a while. After working in this project by myself for about three months, they've allocated more junior developers to assist me on delivering it in time - and I'm currently seen as the lead of the team due to experience compared to them; it means that for more complex tasks, I'm the one to assist them and report back progress to the Engineering Manager. My work in this project, besides guiding other developers, is being in direct involvement with the client to figure out product changes, and then implement layouts, visual elements as well as the logic and integrations.
